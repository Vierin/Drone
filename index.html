What is the Mavic 2 Pro?

The Mavic 2 Pro drone is an engineering marvel ideal for aerial photography. Equipped with all the best DJI technology, the drone will transform the world of aerial photography.

The Mavic 2 Pro features the all-new Hasselblad L1D-20c camera. L1D-20c camera works with Hasselblad's unique Natural Color Solution (HNCS) 5 technology, allowing users to Take great aerial shots with 20 megapixels and stunning colors.
